It turns out hackers can exploit these default passwords to gain free access to your router and therefore all of your internet traffic! It is very much worth changing these default credentials to something more secure. Bad ideas for passwords include personal information, favorite sports teams, and anything someone could learn about you on Facebook. Strong passwords usually contain some mixed-case letters, symbols, and are 10+ characters in length. Another strategy is to use 4 unrelated words that you can remember using a mnemonic sentence, as this is actually very secure. Never use the password "password" or "12345678" etc.
