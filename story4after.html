It is a terrible idea to connect to "Free Public WiFi". The lack of a password means anyone on the network can view your internet traffic. Even worse, a hacker could be running the network and have a program logging your traffic and viewing various login information sent over the network. Worse still, the hacker could set up the network to infect all of the devices that connect to it with malware! Be very, very wary of free wifi in general, and if you have to connect to it, use a VPN.
